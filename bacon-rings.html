Bacon Rings

Price : R50

Serving size : 2


Bacon Wrapped Onion Rings are the perfect appetizer. Spicy red onions wrapped in smoky bacon cooked to perfection in the oven. Best served with BBQ sauce! Instructions · Cut onions into 1/2 inch thick rings and pop the internal rings out until you have a nice sturdy onion ring. · Brush onion with hot sauce. STEP 1. Peel and slice the onion. Pop out the rings. · STEP 2. Wrap the thinly sliced pancetta around the onion ring, secure end with a cocktail stick if need be.
