Blini Platter

Price : R60

Serving size : 2


This smoked salmon platter is a classic appetizer or brunch spread featuring blini or bagels, capers, chives and a fresh dill cream cheese. To reheat them for a party, pop them on a baking tray to warm through in the oven. Make your own #blinis with party-style toppings. Blinis are simple and delicious Russian-style pancakes that are thin like crepes , lay on plate lined with a paper towel to help soak up excess butter.
