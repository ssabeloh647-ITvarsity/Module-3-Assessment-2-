Prawn And Ginger Dumplings

Price : R60

Serving size : 2


These delicious little prawn-filled dumplings are fried to golden and crispy on the one side and remain juicy and tender on the other side. Combine prawns, garlic, chives, chilli, oyster sauce and sesame oil in a bowl. Place wrappers on a flat surface. Spoon 2 teaspoons mince mixture onto the centre of each wrapper. Brush edges with cold water
